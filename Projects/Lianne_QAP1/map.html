Painted Bunting (Passerina ciris)

Map for Painted Bunting Locations


The Painted Bunting Passerina ciris is mostly located in the south region of North America.
Seen mostly in southern United States, Mexico, Guatamala, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Cuba, and Panama.
On rare occasions can be spotted in Canada during summer periods.



Migrational Pattern Map


Painted Buntings Passerina ciris are migrational birds, spending their summers up north in the United States for breeding purposes,
and migrating for winter where they can be found in Mexico, Guatamala, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Cuba, and Panama.
